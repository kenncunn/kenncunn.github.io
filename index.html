Frequently Asked Questions

How can I best contact Miss. Kim?

Sending an email to kcunningham@musd.net is the best way! You can also submit a message/email on the contact page of this website.

Where can I see class pictures?

Class pictures can be viewed on the Morning/Afternoon Class pages. Click either one you'd like to view and enjoy the slideshow of your students!

What if I don't want my student to be posted?

Please send a message to Miss Kim and it will be removed in a timely manner!

Can I share this page with friends and family?

Of course! This page was designed to allow parents, friends, and family of the students to see their progress and pictures!
